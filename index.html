Weather permitting, the ceremony will be outdoors and the reception will include both indoor and outdoor spaces.

Each mailed invitation assumes up to two adult guests. In addition to those two adults, children are also welcome to join.

Please note the celebration will be adult-oriented and will not include kid-specific entertainment or activities. Parents should plan accordingly based on what will be most enjoyable for their family.

If children will be attending, please list their ages in the RSVP notes so we can include them in our planning.
